⚠️ Important Disclaimers

Not Medical Advice

Aperioesca is a meal awareness tool, NOT a medical device or nutrition counseling service. The app provides general information about meal energy density and eating patterns. This information is:

  • ❌ NOT medical advice
  • ❌ NOT a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment
  • ❌ NOT intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease
  • ❌ NOT suitable for managing medical conditions (diabetes, eating disorders, etc.)
Always consult a qualified healthcare provider or registered dietitian before making dietary changes, especially if you have medical conditions or are taking medications.

AI Limitations

Our AI analysis provides estimates based on visual information. Results may be inaccurate due to:

  • Hidden ingredients
  • Portion size estimation errors
  • Food preparation methods
  • Image quality
Do not rely on this app for precise nutritional information.

Introduction

Aperioesca ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

Data We Collect

Photos (Temporarily Processed, Not Stored)

  • What: Meal photos you capture with the camera
  • How: Photos are sent to Google Gemini AI for analysis
  • Storage: Photos are NOT stored on our servers or by Google
  • Processing: Analyzed in real-time and immediately discarded
  • Local Copy: A copy remains on your device for your meal history
Important: Photos are sent for analysis but never permanently stored by us or Google.

Local Data (Stored on Your Device Only)

  • Meal Records: Analysis results, timestamps, and meal history
  • Preferences: App settings and theme choices
Important: This data NEVER leaves your device. We do not have access to it.

Anonymous Usage Data

  • Install ID: A random identifier generated on first launch
  • AI Request Count: Number of analysis requests for rate limiting
  • Timestamp: When requests are made
Purpose: This anonymous data is used ONLY for:
  • Enforcing rate limits (10 analyses per day)
  • Managing AI usage costs
  • No personal identification possible

Data We Do NOT Collect

We do NOT collect:

  • ❌ Your name, email, or contact information
  • ❌ Device identifiers (IMEI, MAC address, etc.)
  • ❌ Location data
  • ❌ Browsing history
  • ❌ Any personally identifiable information

Third-Party Services

Supabase (Backend Infrastructure)

  • What: Hosts our AI proxy service
  • Data Shared: Anonymous install ID, request timestamps
  • Privacy Policy: https://supabase.com/privacy

Google Gemini AI

  • What: Analyzes meal photos
  • Data Shared: Meal photos (temporarily, for analysis only)
  • Processing: Photos are analyzed and immediately discarded
  • Not Stored: Google does not store your photos
  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

How We Use Your Data

1. Meal Analysis: - You take a photo → Photo sent to our Supabase server → Forwarded to Google Gemini AI - AI analyzes the photo and returns energy band estimate - Photo is immediately discarded (not stored by us or Google) - Analysis result saved locally on your device

2. Rate Limiting: Anonymous install ID tracks daily usage (10 requests/day limit)

3. App Functionality: Local storage enables meal history and insights

Data Retention

  • Local Data: Stored on your device until you delete the app
  • Server Data: Anonymous usage logs retained for 90 days for monitoring
  • Photos: Processed in real-time, immediately discarded, NEVER stored on our servers or by Google

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete Data: Uninstall the app to remove all local data
  • Reset ID: Reinstalling generates a new anonymous ID
  • No Account: No login means no account data to manage

Data Security

  • Local-First: All personal data stays on your device
  • Encrypted Transit: API requests use HTTPS encryption
  • No Tracking: We don't track your behavior or identity
  • Minimal Collection: We only collect what's necessary for functionality

Children's Privacy

Aperioesca does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app is rated 4+ and contains no inappropriate content.
